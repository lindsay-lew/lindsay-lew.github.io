About the Labradoodle Breed

The Labradoodle is a cross between a Labrador Retriever and a Poodle. These dogs are known for their intelligence, friendly nature, and social personality, making them well-suited for a variety of living situations and excellent companions for families.

Many Labradoodles inherit the Poodle’s hypoallergenic, low-shedding coat, which is especially appealing to allergy sufferers. Their fur varies in texture from wavy to curly and comes in a range of colors, including cream, chocolate, black, and apricot.

Labradoodles come in 3 different sizes. If you are interested in exploring the differences between the 3 sizes, reference the table below.

Labradoodle Type Height Weight
Miniature 14–16 inches tall 15–30 pounds
Medium 17–20 inches tall 30–45 pounds
Standard 21–24 inches tall 50–65 pounds
